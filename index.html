Experiences You Can Live With Us

Organic & Local Flavors

Organic and local flavors play a key role in the hotel’s guest experience. The culinary concept is based on fresh, seasonal ingredients that celebrate the rich food culture of Gilan. Guests can taste fresh, organic ingredients sourced directly from our garden and local farms, creating authentic farm-to-table dining experiences. By supporting local producers and traditional recipes, the hotel promotes sustainability, wellness, and mindful consumption.

Nature Immersion

Nature immersion defines the core philosophy of our boutique hotel in Gilan. Located within lush forests and misty landscapes, the hotel offers guests an opportunity to disconnect from urban life and experience nature in a slow, mindful, and meaningful way. Through architecture inspired by local traditions, natural materials, and serene outdoor spaces, the hotel encourages relaxation, well-being, and a deep emotional connection with the surrounding environment. Here, nature is not just a backdrop, but an essential part of the guest experience.

Wellness and Slow Living

Wellness and slow living are central to the hotel’s philosophy. The property offers spaces designed for relaxation, meditation, yoga, and mindful living, allowing guests to slow down, reconnect with themselves, and restore both body and mind. Surrounded by nature, every space encourages balance, tranquility, and a deeper sense of well-being, transforming the stay into a holistic and rejuvenating experience

Indoor Garden Space

The hotel features a thoughtfully designed indoor garden space that brings nature into the heart of the building. This green interior creates a calm and refreshing atmosphere, enhancing relaxation and well-being throughout the year. By offering a peaceful retreat in every season, the indoor garden allows guests to stay connected with nature even while enjoying indoor comfort.

About Green Palace

Green Palace was created with a clear vision: to offer a refined hospitality experience deeply connected to nature, sustainability, and human well-being. More than a place to stay, Green Palace is a destination designed for guests who seek tranquility, authenticity, and meaningful experiences away from the pace of urban life. Located in the lush natural landscapes of northern Iran, Green Palace blends contemporary comfort with eco-conscious design. Every element of the project—from architecture and materials to services and guest experiences—has been carefully planned to respect the environment while ensuring a high standard of comfort and privacy. Sustainability is at the core of our philosophy. The project integrates renewable energy solutions, responsible resource management, and environmentally friendly practices that reduce ecological impact without compromising quality. This commitment allows guests to enjoy a luxurious stay while contributing to a more sustainable future. Green Palace also embraces the concept of slow living and wellness. Our spaces are designed to encourage relaxation, mindfulness, and reconnection with nature. Whether through peaceful surroundings, organic cuisine, or thoughtfully designed interiors, we aim to create an atmosphere where guests can truly unwind and feel at ease. With a boutique approach and a limited number of accommodations, we offer personalized hospitality that values genuine human connection. At Green Palace, every guest is welcomed with care, attention, and respect, ensuring an intimate and memorable stay rooted in comfort, nature, and authenticity.
